# New App Brainstorm

A companion app for my trip to Prague, Vienna, and Budapest.

## Main View:

* Header with the city name: Philly, Prague, Vienna, or Budapest
  * Philly will be bookends, both at the start of the trip and at the end of the return home.The main focus is prague, vienna, Budapest.
  * Toggle switches the city page.
* Google Maps
* WHAT TO DO tab
* WHAT TO SAY tab
* currency calculator
  * Defaults to an exchange of local currency to USD, 2-way conversion within the UI
* Itinerary
  * Visually pleasing but not interactive, let's make it easy.
  * Think timeline with nodes as you scroll down
  * Itinerary items
    * Item name (examples: Prague Castle; Eat a chimney cake)
    * time
    * Link to a relevant item. examples: google maps link; ticket confirmation link; informational website/content.
    * Notes - very brief, if any at all.
* WHAT TO DO tab
  * WHAT TO DO header
  * filter button
  * Table of activities
    * name
    * Location
    * Estimate of time needed
    * Cost
    * Notes
    * Link to relevant item
    * Priority out of 5
* WHAT TO SAY
  * WHAT TO SAY header
  * Priminent search Bar
  * Sections or buttons, some place for these categories: Basic, Food, Travel, Getting Help, Other
  * each category has 2 sub items: Words, and info
    * Words are common phrases and translations from English to the local language and vice versa. A language reference on the go.
    * Info is informational content. For example, under Food/Info I could write about tipping culture in that city. Reference material not related to the language.
* Memories
  * This set of features aim to capture this trip as a memory.
  * The itinerary, where we stayed, what we did, where we ate should be there or at least easily captured. Image content should be encouraged. tidbits of text diary entries, reviews, notes, that sort of thing from both me and from Niko, my gf and travel companion.
  * Need to further ideate on this, but it will become an important part
  * No output is needed within the app. The main purpose is capture and collection. We can process afterwards into a deliverable artifact. In the meantime, if there is an opportunity for nice UI to reflect new content, not just an upload screen, let's do it.
